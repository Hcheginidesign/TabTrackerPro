Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [06/12/2024]

Introduction

Welcome to TabTrackerPro! Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Chrome extension.

What Information We Collect

TabTrackerPro does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. All operations are performed locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

TabTrackerPro processes your data solely for its intended functionality:

  • Tracking visited tabs and URLs during your browsing session.
  • Allowing you to download or upload a list of URLs for personal use.

All processing occurs locally on your device, and no data is shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

TabTrackerPro does not interact with any third-party services, APIs, or external servers.

Permissions

TabTrackerPro requires the following permissions for its functionality:

  • tabs: To retrieve and manage information about open tabs for tracking and reopening URLs.
  • downloads: To enable users to save a list of URLs as a `.txt` file.
  • storage: To save user settings (like theme preferences) and tab-related data across sessions.

These permissions are used solely for their stated purposes and are not used to collect or transmit data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
